Meet the Designer
Scott Brownlie.

Hi, I'm Scott, founder of Scott Brownlie Interiors, a brand new interior design company based in Crawley, West Sussex.

I established my design service in 2016 after recently graduating from The National Design Academy with a diploma in Interior Design. I have a passion for everything Interior Design and I believe my love for this art form is reflected in the work I do. Originally from sunny Glasgow, I moved to the south 10 years ago to take a career with the airlines. I've been incredibly lucky to have seen some pretty amazing things all over the world, and met so many different and diverse people. I believe that this background gives me the edge when it comes to my design service. Whether you are a contemporary fan or something more traditional, I will work with you to transform your current interiors with fresh, and on trend styles. Take a look on The Portfolio page for recent work as well as The Design Ideas page for inspiration. And don't forget to check back regularly for further updates. I look forward to hearing from you soon!
